Academic Mission & Vision

Why Robotics

Robotics represents the ultimate integration of theory and practice—where mathematics meets mechanics, and algorithms become physical action. I'm drawn to its potential to solve tangible human problems.

Values & Motivation

My work is guided by perseverance, ethical responsibility, and a commitment to accessibility. I believe technology should empower, not exclude—especially in healthcare applications.

Long-term Vision

To lead a research group developing adaptive, affordable rehabilitation robotics for global deployment. I aim to contribute to both technical innovation and policy for equitable technology access.
