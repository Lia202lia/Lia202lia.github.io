My research focuses on creating adaptive robotic systems that bridge the gap between human capability and technological assistance, with particular emphasis on rehabilitation robotics and intelligent control methodologies.

Research Focus Areas

Human–Robot Interaction

Designing intuitive interfaces and adaptive behaviors for seamless collaboration between humans and robotic systems in therapeutic and assistive contexts.

Rehabilitation Robotics

Developing exoskeleton systems and assistive devices with adaptive control algorithms for neurorehabilitation and mobility restoration.

Intelligent Control

Implementing fuzzy logic, neural networks, and hybrid control strategies for autonomous systems in unstructured environments.

Computer Vision

Applying machine learning and image processing techniques for environmental perception and human motion analysis in robotic systems.
