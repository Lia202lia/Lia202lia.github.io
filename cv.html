Research & Projects

Hand Exoskeleton for Rehabilitation

Jan 2024 – Aug 2025
  • Designed and dynamic modeling of a self-alignment, lightweight, cable-driven hand exoskeleton for safe human-robot interaction
  • Implemented three control strategies (Adaptive SMC, DMRAC, PID with NN compensation) for the nonlinear model
  • Achieved <0.5° tracking error and 20% reduction in settling time under disturbances
  • Applied stereo vision–based hand pose mirroring, reducing mean joint position error from 22.4 mm to 15.4 mm
  • Maintained system latency <200 ms for real-time operation
  • Technologies: MATLAB/Simulink, ROS, SolidWorks, Python (TensorFlow), Adaptive Control

Fuzzy Logic Based Control of a Unicycle Mobile Robot - Comparative Analysis

Nov 2023 – Feb 2024
  • Designed, implemented, and fine-tuned four Mamdani-type fuzzy controllers (P, PI, PD, PID-like)
  • Achieved high accuracy in goal tracking: PID-like fuzzy control reached 0.001 m error with negligible oscillations
  • PI-like controller achieved 0.05 m error, PD-like 0.1 m error
  • Demonstrated robustness and smooth trajectory following in simulated environments
  • Technologies: Python, MATLAB, Fuzzy Logic Toolbox, Robotics Simulation
