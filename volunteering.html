Philosophy of Service

My volunteering is an extension of my academic identity. I view service not as a separate activity, but as an integral part of being an engineer and a scholar. It is through mentoring, sharing knowledge, and engaging with community needs that technical skills find their ethical grounding and true purpose. These experiences have shaped my resolve to pursue research that is not only innovative but also socially conscious and broadly accessible.
