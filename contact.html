Frequently Asked Questions

What should I include in a scholarship reference request?

Please provide: 1) The scholarship name and institution, 2) Application deadline, 3) Specific requirements for the reference letter, 4) Your CV and personal statement, 5) Any particular aspects of your work you'd like emphasized.

Can you review my research proposal or application materials?

I offer limited review for students from underrepresented regions or working on humanitarian robotics projects. Please include your draft and specific areas where you seek feedback.

Are you available for internships or short-term collaborations?

I consider remote research collaborations and internships, particularly in rehabilitation robotics and intelligent control. Please describe the project scope, timeline, and expected outcomes.

What's the best way to stay updated on your research?

Follow my GitHub for code releases and LinkedIn for research updates and publications. I periodically share project summaries and technical insights.
