Academic Vision & Research Philosophy

My work is driven by the conviction that robotics should extend human capabilities, not replace them. I focus on creating systems that are not just technically sophisticated, but also intuitive, accessible, and responsive to human needs.

Through rehabilitation robotics and intelligent control systems, I aim to bridge the gap between theoretical innovation and tangible human benefit. My approach combines rigorous engineering with interdisciplinary insights from neuroscience, human factors, and ethics.
